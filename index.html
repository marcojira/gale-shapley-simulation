In a bizarre turn of events, Sauron has joined forces with Voldemort. To combat this new enemy, the protoganists of both universes have decided to join forces. Before the great battle, it is decided that each wizard should pair up with one member of the Fellowship of the Ring to make sure each group has knowledge of both enemies.

Time is of the essence and unfortunately both groups cannot seem to come up with an optimal set of pairings. Luckily, just as hope dwindled, an ancient text is found: "College Admissions and the Stability of Marriage" by D. Gale and L. S. Shapley...
